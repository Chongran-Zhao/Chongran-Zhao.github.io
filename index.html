Biography

I am currently completing my Master of Engineering in Mechanics at the Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech), advised by Prof. Ju Liu.

Starting in September 2026, I will join Brown University as a Ph.D. student in Engineering in the Mechanics of Solids and Structures Group. I expect to work with Prof. Haneesh Kesari in the Applied Mechanics Lab.

Previously, I obtained my Bachelor of Science degree in Theoretical and Applied Mechanics from SUSTech (09/2019 - 06/2023).

Research Focus

1. Finite-Strain Viscoelasticity Theory

I develop finite-strain viscoelasticity theories grounded in Green-Naghdi kinematics and generalized strain measures to capture nonlinear, time-dependent deformation.

2. Model Calibration

We calibrate hyperelastic and viscoelastic models to experiments and include damage effects (Mullins effect) when necessary.

3. Finite Element Simulation

We implement these models in our group’s open-source solver PERIGEE for finite element simulations and validation.
